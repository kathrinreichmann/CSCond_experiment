###### Lernphase

## conditioning phase
- simultaneous presentation duration: 2000 ms
- inter-trial-interval: 500 ms

## Präsentationshäufigkeit
#one-to-one

- 4 categories
- select 1 CS per Category
- 4 individual CS in learning phase
- show individual CS 5 times per block (total: 20 times) without direct repetition of same stimulus
- show four blocks (total: 80 times)

#one-to-many

- 4 categories
- select 5 CS per Category
- 20 individual CS in learning phase
- show individual CS 1 times per block (total: 20 times) without direct repetetition of same stimulus
- show four blocks (total: 80 times)

####### Randomisierung
- random assignment of 2xpos and 2xneg valence to categorie 1-4
- randomized stimulus presentation
- randomized selection of 1 US for each CS
- randomized selection of 1 (5) CSs and 3 GS out of a pool of 10 CSs
- random assignment to direct vs. indirect rating first
- random assignment to one-to-one vs. many-to-one
